The Future of Writing is Green

SproutScribe is the world's first fully biodegradable pen that grows into plants after use.

Learn More Our Research

About SproutScribe

SproutScribe is a revolutionary writing instrument designed by high school students as part of a science research project. Our mission is to reduce plastic waste in the writing instrument industry while creating a product that gives back to nature.

Traditional pens contribute significantly to plastic pollution, with billions ending up in landfills each year. SproutScribe addresses this problem with an innovative solution — a pen that biodegrades completely and can even grow into plants when planted.

“After 6 months of decomposition testing, our prototype showed 92% biodegradation compared to ~0% for conventional plastic pens.”

Innovative Features

100% Biodegradable

Made from plant-based materials that can decompose naturally within ~6 months when composted.

Grows Into Plants

Cap contains non‑GMO seeds that sprout after planting once the pen’s life is over.

Smooth Writing

High‑quality plant‑based ink that flows smoothly and is safe for the environment.

The Science Behind SproutScribe

Materials Research

After testing numerous biodegradable materials, we developed a proprietary blend of PLA (polylactic acid) from corn starch reinforced with bamboo fibers for durability. The ink is derived from vegetable dyes and natural pigments.

Decomposition Timeline

0–2 months~15% degraded
2–4 months~60% degraded
4–6 months~92% degraded

Testing Methodology

We conducted controlled decomposition tests in three environments: industrial compost, home compost, and landfill conditions. Our results show optimal decomposition in composting environments.

Key Findings

  • No toxic residues left after decomposition
  • Seed germination rate of ~78% from cap‑embedded seeds
  • Writing performance comparable to conventional pens
