The Science Behind SproutScribe

Materials Research

We developed a proprietary blend of PLA (polylactic acid) derived from corn starch, reinforced with bamboo fibers. The ink is based on vegetable pigments for safety and biodegradability.

Decomposition Timeline:

0–2 months: 15% degraded

2–4 months: 60% degraded

4–6 months: 92% degraded

Testing Methodology

Controlled decomposition tests were carried out in three environments: industrial compost, home compost, and landfill conditions. Optimal degradation occurred in compost settings.

  • No toxic residues left after decomposition
  • Seed germination success: 78%
  • Writing performance equal to conventional pens
